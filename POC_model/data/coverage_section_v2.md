# NCD 210.14 — Coverage Indications (v2, CAG-00439R, effective 2022-02-10)

## Section ID: NCD-210.14-COVERAGE-V2

Medicare covers screening for lung cancer with low dose computed tomography (LDCT) once per year when all of the following criteria are met:

1. The beneficiary must be Age 50 - 77 years.

2. The beneficiary must have a tobacco smoking history of at least 20 pack-years.

3. The beneficiary must be a current smoker or one who has quit smoking within the last 15 years.

4. The beneficiary must be asymptomatic (no signs or symptoms of lung cancer).

5. The beneficiary must receive a written order for LDCT lung cancer screening during a lung cancer screening counseling and shared decision-making visit (HCPCS G0296) prior to the first screening.

6. Covered procedure codes include HCPCS G0296 (counseling/SDM visit) and CPT 71271 (CT thorax low dose for lung cancer screening).
